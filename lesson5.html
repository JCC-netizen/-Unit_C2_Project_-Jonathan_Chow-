Lesson 5: Hyperlinks and Images

Hyperlinks (<a>) are used to connect pages or websites. The <img> tag is used to display images on a webpage. Both of these elements are fundamental for interactive, media-rich web pages.

Example HTML Code:

        <a href="https://www.example.com">Click here to visit Example</a>
        <img src="logo.png" alt="Website Logo">

On the webpage, the hyperlink will create a clickable link, and the image will be displayed on the page.
