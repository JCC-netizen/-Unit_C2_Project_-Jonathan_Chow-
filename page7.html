Lesson 6: Hyperlinks

Hyperlinks <a> are used to connect pages or websites. These links can connect to other web pages, files, email addresses, or even specific parts of the same page. The most important attribute of the <a> tag is the href attribute, which specifies the URL of the page the link goes to.

Example of <a> tag:

        <a href="https://www.example.com">Click here to visit Example</a>

On the webpage, the hyperlink will create a clickable link, and the image will be displayed on the page.
